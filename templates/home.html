Welcome to RetroBookStore

Welcome to RetroBookStore, the online library management system that lets you explore, borrow, and review books of your choice. Whether you are looking for fiction, non-fiction, or any other genre, you can find it here at RetroBookStore. To get started, you need to create an account and deposit some money to your Bookworm wallet. You can use this money to borrow books for a fixed period of time. You will receive an email confirmation after every transaction. You can also return books and get your money back. As a RetroBookStore user, you can also rate and review the books you have read. You can share your opinions and feedback with other readers and discover new books based on their recommendations. You can also view your borrowing history and profile details on your dashboard.
